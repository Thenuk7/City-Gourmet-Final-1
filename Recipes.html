Featured Recipe: Sushi Rolls

  1. Prepare Sushi Rice

    • Rinse and cook 2 cups of sushi rice.
    • Mix 1/3 cup rice vinegar, 3 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp salt. Heat and stir until dissolved.
    • Mix the seasoning with the cooked rice and let it cool.

  2. Prepare Fillings

    • Slice cucumbers, avocados, and other vegetables into thin strips.
    • Cut fish or crab sticks into thin strips.

  3. Assemble Sushi Rolls

    • Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat.
    • Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
    • Arrange fillings near the bottom edge of the rice.
    • Roll the sushi using the mat, pressing firmly to shape it.
    • Wet the top border of the nori and seal the roll.

  4. Slice and Serve

    • Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
    • Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.

Featured Recipe - Lasagna

  1. Prep Ingredients:

    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cook lasagna noodles and set aside.

  2. Make Meat Sauce (Optional):

    • Brown ground beef or Italian sausage in olive oil.
    • Season and add tomato sauce. Simmer.

  3. Assembly:

    • Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat layers.
    • Top with grated Parmesan cheese.

  4. Baking:

    • Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 mins.
    • Remove foil and bake uncovered for 10-15 mins until cheese is golden.

  5. Tips:

    • Customize with veggies or different meats.
    • Ensure meat is fully cooked.
    • Can be prepared ahead and refrigerated.
    • Serve with salad and garlic bread.
